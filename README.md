RetailEase is an **AI-powered inventory management platform** designed for **pharmacies and small retail stores**.  
It helps businesses monitor inventory in real time, prevent expiry losses, predict stock shortages, and improve profitability using **AI-driven insights**.

---

## 📌 Problem

Pharmacies frequently experience inventory challenges such as:

- Medicines expiring unnoticed  
- Sudden stock shortages  
- Manual inventory tracking errors  
- Hidden profit leakage  

These issues lead to **financial loss and inefficient inventory decisions**.

---

## 💡 Solution

RetailEase provides an intelligent platform that helps pharmacies:

- Track inventory in real time
- Receive alerts before medicines expire
- Predict stock shortages using AI
- Monitor profit and revenue through dashboards
- Make smarter purchasing decisions

The goal is to **reduce medicine wastage and increase pharmacy profits**.
